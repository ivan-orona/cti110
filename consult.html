Consultation

Setting up an appointment is critical in the bike building process.
After scheduling an appointment (see below for details), you will
be contacted in a prompt manner and speak with one of our team members.


They will then ask a short series of questions such as the model of
your bike and what you intend to go for in terms of performance and
aesthetics. During the actual appointment, we'll run through some custom
work that has already been done to previous motorcycles and help you
form an idea and get a estimate of labor and costs. The first step is
making that call! You'll never know unless you visit!
