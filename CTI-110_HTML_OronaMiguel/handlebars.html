Handlebars

Handlebars are usually one of the first things we recommend riders look into changing out. Stock handlebars
tend to be rigid, stiff, uncomfortable, and fixed at a height that might force you into a position that puts you in
danger. We first like to sit you on a bike that has the handlebars removed, and have you place your hands in
the air you believe woule be most fitting.


The second step is to pick out a style that you think would be compliment the style your aiming for.
We would also like to caution to not be the rider who 'buys cheap now, get expensive later'. It is in our
professional opinion that with a price tag comes a trusted name, and a safer investment.
